# Airdrops

Receive token airdrops and participate in campaigns on RetroChat.

## Receiving Airdrops

### Eligibility

Airdrops may be distributed to:
- Active community members
- Early adopters
- Contest winners
- Specific user groups
- All users (community airdrops)

### Notification

When you're eligible for an airdrop:
- You'll receive a notification
- Check your airdrop history
- View airdrop details and amount

### Claiming Airdrops

1. Go to your wallet settings
2. Navigate to "Airdrops"
3. View available airdrops
4. Click "Claim" on eligible airdrops
5. Confirm in your wallet
6. Tokens are sent to your wallet

### Automatic Distribution

Some airdrops are automatically distributed:
- No claim needed
- Tokens appear in your wallet
- Notification sent upon completion

## Airdrop Types

### Community Airdrops
- Distributed to all active users
- Reward for platform participation
- Regular distribution schedule

### Contest Airdrops
- Rewards for contest winners
- Specific recipient selection
- Custom amounts per winner

### Referral Airdrops
- Rewards for referring new users
- Bonus for successful referrals
- Track referral earnings

### Special Event Airdrops
- Limited-time distributions
- Event participation rewards
- Exclusive to event attendees

## Requirements

### Wallet Connection
- Must have a connected Solana wallet
- Wallet must be active
- Valid wallet address required

### Account Status
- Active account
- Verified email (for some airdrops)
- No account restrictions

## Security

### Verification
- Always verify airdrop legitimacy
- Official airdrops come from RetroChat
- Never share your seed phrase
- Be cautious of scams

### Scam Prevention
- RetroChat will never ask for your seed phrase
- Official airdrops are free
- No payment required to claim
- Report suspicious airdrops

## Tips

1. **Stay Active**: Active users are more likely to receive airdrops
2. **Connect Wallet**: Ensure your wallet is connected
3. **Check Regularly**: Monitor your airdrop history
4. **Participate**: Join contests and events
5. **Refer Friends**: Earn referral airdrops

## Troubleshooting

**Didn't receive expected airdrop?**
- Check eligibility requirements
- Verify wallet is connected
- Check airdrop history
- Contact support

**Can't claim airdrop?**
- Verify wallet connection
- Check account status
- Ensure sufficient SOL for gas
- Try refreshing the page

**Airdrop amount seems wrong?**
- Check airdrop details
- Verify eligibility tier
- Contact support for clarification
