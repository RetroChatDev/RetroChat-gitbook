# Getting Started with RetroChat

Welcome to RetroChat! This guide will help you set up your account and start chatting in minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [RetroChat](https://retrochat.io) in your web browser.
2. Click **Sign Up** on the login screen.
3. Enter your email address and choose a password.
4. Verify your email if prompted.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

After signing in for the first time, you'll be guided through profile setup:

1. **Choose a Screen Name** — Pick something fun and unique, or use the built-in Screen Name Generator for retro-inspired suggestions.
2. **Select an Avatar** — Choose from a library of classic-style avatars.
3. **Enter Your Date of Birth** — Required for age verification (you must be at least 13).
4. **Add Social Links** *(optional)* — Link your Twitter/X, Discord, or other profiles.
5. **Accept the Terms of Service** — Review and agree to continue.

## Step 3: Join a Chat Room

Once your profile is set up, you'll land on the main chat lobby:

- **Browse Rooms** — Scroll through available public rooms or search by name/tag.
- **Join a Room** — Click any room card to enter. Some rooms may require a password.
- **Create a Room** — Click the "Create Room" button to start your own. You can make it public or private, set a description, add tags, and even choose a wallpaper.

## Step 4: Start Chatting

Inside a chat room:

- Type your message in the input bar and press **Enter** to send.
- Use the **Emoticon Picker** 😊 to add classic emoticons.
- Use the **GIF Picker** to share animated GIFs.
- **Reply** to messages by clicking the reply button on any message.
- **Attach files** using the file attachment button (images, documents, etc.).
- **Mention users** by typing `@` followed by their screen name.

## Step 5: Explore More Features

RetroChat is much more than chat rooms! Once you're comfortable:

- 🌐 **Communities** — Join or create communities with posts, live broadcasts, and voice spaces.
- 📺 **Retro TV** — Watch curated retro content from the TV player in the sidebar.
- 📻 **Radio** — Listen to live radio stations while you chat.
- 💸 **Web3 Features** — Connect your Solana wallet to unlock tipping, airdrops, DeFi tracking, and the Token Launchpad.
- 🎮 **Retro Arcade** — Coming soon!
