# Create Your Account

Creating a RetroChat account is quick and easy. You have multiple options to get started.

## Sign-Up Methods

### Email and Password

1. Click "Sign Up" on the RetroChat homepage
2. Enter your email address
3. Choose a secure password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile setup

**Requirements:**
- Valid email address
- Password must be at least 8 characters
- You must be at least 16 years old

### Google OAuth

1. Click "Sign Up with Google"
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant necessary permissions
4. Complete your profile setup

### Solana Wallet

1. Click "Connect Wallet"
2. Select your wallet provider (Phantom or Solflare)
3. Approve the connection
4. Complete your profile setup

**Note:** Wallet sign-up automatically links your wallet to your account for Web3 features.

## Profile Setup

After creating your account, you'll be prompted to:

1. Choose a username (must be unique)
2. Upload an avatar (optional)
3. Add a bio (optional)
4. Set your display preferences

## Account Verification

- Email accounts: Check your inbox for a verification link
- Wallet accounts: Verification is automatic
- Google accounts: Verification is automatic

## Troubleshooting

**Can't receive verification email?**
- Check your spam folder
- Verify the email address is correct
- Request a new verification email

**Username already taken?**
- Try a variation with numbers or underscores
- Usernames are case-sensitive

**Having trouble connecting your wallet?**
- Make sure your wallet extension is installed and unlocked
- Check our [Wallet Connection Troubleshooting](../web3-integration/solana-wallet-setup.md) guide
