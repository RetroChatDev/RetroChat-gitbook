# Airdrops & Tipping

## SOL Tipping

RetroChat allows users to send SOL (Solana) tips directly to other users as a way to show appreciation, support creators, or reward great conversations.

### How to Send a Tip

1. Click on a user's profile or the tip button next to their message.
2. The **Tip Modal** will open.
3. Select or enter the amount of SOL you'd like to send.
4. Choose the token you want to tip with (SOL or other supported SPL tokens).
5. Confirm the transaction in your connected wallet (Phantom or Solflare).
6. The tip is sent instantly on the Solana blockchain.

### Tip History

- View all tips you've sent and received from the **Tip History** section.
- Each entry shows the sender, recipient, amount, token, and timestamp.

### Tip Leaderboard

RetroChat features a **Tip Leaderboard** that highlights:

- **Top Tippers** — The most generous users on the platform
- **Most Tipped** — Users who have received the most tips

The leaderboard updates in real time and is visible to all users.

### Requirements

- You must have a **Solana wallet connected** (Phantom or Solflare).
- You must have sufficient SOL or token balance to cover the tip amount plus transaction fees.
- Tips are **irreversible** — once confirmed on-chain, they cannot be undone.

## Airdrop Campaigns

Airdrops let you distribute tokens to multiple users at once — perfect for community rewards, promotions, or token launches.

### Creating an Airdrop Campaign

1. Navigate to the **Airdrop Manager** section.
2. Click **Create Campaign**.
3. Fill in the campaign details:
   - **Name** — A descriptive name for the campaign
   - **Description** — What the airdrop is about
   - **Token Mint** — The Solana token address to distribute
   - **Amount Per User** — How much each recipient will receive
   - **Recipients** — Target a specific room's members or define custom criteria
4. Review and save the campaign.

### Executing an Airdrop

- Once a campaign is created, you can review the recipient list.
- Click **Execute** to begin distributing tokens.
- The system will process each recipient and report successful and failed sends.
- You can track progress in real time.

### Campaign Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Campaign created but not yet executed |
| **Executing** | Tokens are being distributed |
| **Completed** | All tokens have been sent |
| **Cancelled** | Campaign was cancelled before or during execution |

### Important Notes

- You must have sufficient tokens and SOL (for transaction fees) in your connected wallet.
- Airdrop transactions are processed on-chain and are **irreversible**.
- Failed sends (e.g., invalid wallet addresses) are logged and can be reviewed.
- RetroChat does not take custody of your tokens at any point — all transactions happen directly from your wallet.
