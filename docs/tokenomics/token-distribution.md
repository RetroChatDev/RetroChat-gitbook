# Token Distribution

Detailed breakdown of RetroChat token distribution.

## Distribution Overview

The total token supply is allocated across multiple categories to ensure fair distribution and long-term sustainability.

## Allocation Breakdown

### Community Rewards (40%)
- Active user rewards
- Content creator incentives
- Referral bonuses
- Contest prizes
- Airdrops

### Development & Operations (25%)
- Platform development
- Team compensation
- Operational costs
- Technology infrastructure

### Liquidity & Exchange (15%)
- DEX liquidity provision
- Exchange listings
- Market making
- Trading pairs

### Staking Rewards (10%)
- Staking pool rewards
- Long-term holder incentives
- Passive income distribution
- Lock-up bonuses

### Reserve Fund (10%)
- Emergency reserves
- Future development
- Strategic partnerships
- Market stabilization

## Distribution Schedule

### Initial Distribution
- Fair launch allocation
- Community airdrops
- Early adopter rewards

### Ongoing Distribution
- Monthly community rewards
- Weekly staking distributions
- Continuous airdrops
- Referral bonuses

### Vesting Schedule
- Team tokens: 4-year vesting
- Development fund: Gradual release
- Reserve fund: Long-term allocation

## Fair Launch

RetroChat follows a fair launch model:
- No pre-sale or private sale
- Equal opportunity for all
- Transparent distribution
- Community-first approach

## Transparency

All distribution information is:
- Publicly available
- On-chain verifiable
- Regularly updated
- Audited and verified
