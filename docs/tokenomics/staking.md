# Staking (Coming Soon)

Staking is coming soon to RetroChat! Earn passive rewards by staking your tokens.

## What is Staking?

Staking allows you to lock your tokens for a period of time to earn rewards while supporting the platform.

## Benefits

### Passive Income
- Earn rewards over time
- Automatic distribution
- Compound rewards
- Flexible options

### Platform Support
- Support platform operations
- Contribute to security

- Long-term commitment

### Additional Perks
- Governance voting rights
- Priority features
- Exclusive access
- Special rewards

## How It Will Work

### Staking Process
1. Navigate to Staking section
2. Choose staking amount
3. Select lock-up period
4. Confirm and stake
5. Start earning rewards

### Lock-up Periods
- Flexible: No lock-up (lower rewards)
- Short-term: 30 days (moderate rewards)
- Medium-term: 90 days (higher rewards)
- Long-term: 180+ days (highest rewards)

### Reward Rates
- Based on lock-up period
- Competitive APY
- Regular distributions
- Compound options
