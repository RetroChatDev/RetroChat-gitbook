# User Guides & How-Tos

Practical guides for getting the most out of RetroChat.

---

## Chat Rooms

### Creating a Chat Room

1. From the main lobby, click **Create Room**.
2. Enter a room name and optional description.
3. Choose visibility: **Public** (anyone can join) or **Private** (password required).
4. Set a password if the room is private.
5. Add tags to help others find your room.
6. Set a maximum user limit if desired.
7. Click **Create** to launch your room.

### Customizing Room Wallpapers

1. Inside a chat room, open **Room Settings**.
2. Navigate to the **Wallpaper** section.
3. Choose from preset wallpapers or enter a custom image URL.
4. The wallpaper will update for all users in the room.

### Sending Messages with Attachments

1. Click the **file attachment** button in the message input area.
2. Select a file from your device (images, documents, etc.).
3. The file will be uploaded and shared in the chat.
4. Images are displayed inline; other files appear as downloadable links.

### Using Emoticons and GIFs

- Click the **smiley face** icon to open the Emoticon Picker and select a classic emoticon.
- Click the **GIF** button to search for and share animated GIFs from GIPHY.

### Replying to Messages

- Hover over (or tap) a message and click the **Reply** button.
- Your reply will be linked to the original message for context.

### Mentioning Users

- Type `@` followed by a user's screen name to mention them.
- The mentioned user will receive a notification.

## Communities

### Creating a Community

1. Go to the **Communities** section.
2. Click **Create Community**.
3. Fill in the name, description, and optional banner image.
4. Add social links (Twitter/X, Discord, website, etc.).
5. Set visibility to Public or Private.
6. Click **Create**.

### Posting in a Community

1. Open a community you're a member of.
2. Use the **Create Post** composer at the top.
3. Add a title, content, and optional media attachments.
4. Click **Post** to publish.

### Starting a Voice Space

1. As a community owner or moderator, click **Start Space**.
2. A voice room will be created for the community.
3. Members can join to listen or request to speak.
4. Manage speakers from the Spaces panel.

### Going Live (Broadcasting)

1. As a community owner or moderator, click **Go Live**.
2. Allow camera and microphone access when prompted.
3. Preview your camera feed before starting.
4. Click **Start Broadcast** to go live.
5. Members will see the live indicator and can tune in.

## Retro TV

### Watching Content

1. Open the **Retro TV** player from the sidebar.
2. Browse channels by category or use the channel selector.
3. Click a channel to start watching.
4. Use the **TV Guide** to see what's available.

### Minimized Player

- While watching, navigate away from the TV section.
- The player will minimize to a small bar at the bottom of the screen.
- Click the mini player to expand it, or close it to stop watching.

---

## Radio

### Finding Stations

1. Open the **Radio** player from the sidebar.
2. Browse stations or use the search bar.
3. Filter by genre, country, or popularity.
4. Click a station to start listening.

### Background Playback

- Radio continues playing as you navigate through RetroChat.
- Use the player controls in the sidebar to pause, stop, or change stations.

---

## Direct Messages

### Sending a Direct Message

1. Click on a user's name or avatar in a chat room or user list.
2. Select **Direct Message** from the profile popup.
3. Type your message and send.

### Viewing Your DMs

- Navigate to the **Direct Messages** tab to see all your conversations.
- Unread messages are highlighted.

---

## Profile Management

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click on your avatar or screen name to open your profile.
2. Edit your screen name, avatar, bio, or social links.
3. Save your changes.

### Connecting Social Media

- In your profile settings, add links to your Twitter/X, Discord, Telegram, or personal website.
- These links will be visible on your public profile.

### Following Users

- Click the **Follow** button on any user's profile.
- View your followers and following lists from your profile.

---

## Favorites

### Favoriting a Room

- Click the **star** icon on any room card to add it to your favorites.
- Access your favorite rooms quickly from the favorites section.

---

## Dark Mode

### Toggling Theme

- Click the **theme toggle** button (sun/moon icon) in the header.
- RetroChat will switch between light and dark modes.
- Your preference is saved automatically.
