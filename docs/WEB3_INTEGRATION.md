# Web3 Integration

RetroChat is built with Web3 at its core, providing a seamless bridge between social interaction and the Solana blockchain.

---

## Wallet Connection

Connect your Solana wallet to unlock the full RetroChat experience.

### Supported Wallets

- **Phantom** — The most popular Solana wallet

### How to Connect

1. Click the **Wallet** button in the app header or sidebar.
2. Select your preferred wallet (Phantom or Solflare).
3. Approve the connection in your wallet app.
4. Your wallet address will appear on your profile.

### Disconnecting

You can disconnect your wallet at any time from the wallet menu. Disconnecting does not affect your RetroChat account — you simply won't be able to use Web3 features until you reconnect.

## DeFi Portfolio

View your Solana token holdings directly within RetroChat.

- **Token balances** — See all SPL tokens in your connected wallet
- **Price tracking** — Real-time price data powered by Jupiter and DexScreener
- **Portfolio value** — View your total portfolio value in USD

Browse your NFT collection without leaving RetroChat.

## Price Alerts

Stay on top of the market with customizable price alerts.

- **Set alerts** — Choose a token and set a target price
- **Notifications** — Get notified when your target price is hit
- **Manage alerts** — View, edit, or delete your active alerts

## IPFS Storage

- **Pinning** — Files are pinned to ensure availability
- **Gateway access** — Access your files through multiple IPFS gateways
- **Pin health monitoring** — Track the health and availability of your pinned files

## Important Notes

- RetroChat **never** has access to your private keys.
- All transactions require your explicit wallet approval.
- Blockchain transactions are **irreversible** — double-check before confirming.
- RetroChat does not provide financial advice. All investment decisions are your own.
- Gas fees (SOL) are required for on-chain transactions.
