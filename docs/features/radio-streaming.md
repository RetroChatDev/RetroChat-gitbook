# Radio Streaming

Discover and share music through RetroChat's radio streaming feature.

## Browse Stations

Explore a curated selection of radio stations.

### Station Categories

- **Genre Stations**: Rock, Pop, Electronic, Hip-Hop, and more
- **Mood Stations**: Chill, Energetic, Relaxing, Focus
- **Community Stations**: User-created stations
- **Featured Stations**: Editor's picks

### Finding Stations

1. Navigate to the Radio section
2. Browse by category or search
3. Click on a station to start listening
4. See what others are listening to

### Station Features

- Real-time playback
- Station information and description
- Current track display
- Share stations with friends

## Create Playlists

Build your own music playlists and share them with the community.

### Creating a Playlist

1. Go to the Radio section
2. Click "Create Playlist"
3. Give your playlist a name and description
4. Add tracks or stations
5. Save and share!

### Playlist Management

- Add/remove tracks
- Reorder songs
- Edit playlist details
- Make playlists public or private
- Share playlists with specific users

### Playlist Features

- Custom artwork
- Playlist descriptions
- Follow other users' playlists
- Collaborative playlists (coming soon)

## Listening Experience

### Player Controls

- Play/Pause
- Skip tracks
- Volume control
- Shuffle mode
- Repeat mode

### Integration

- Listen while chatting
- Share what you're listening to
- See what friends are playing
- Discover new music through recommendations

## Sharing

### Share Stations
- Share station links in chat
- Post to your profile
- Recommend to friends

### Share Playlists
- Share playlist links
- Embed in messages
- Export playlists

## Tips

1. **Explore**: Try different stations to discover new music
2. **Create Playlists**: Organize your favorite tracks
3. **Share**: Help others discover great music
4. **Follow**: Follow playlists from users with similar taste
