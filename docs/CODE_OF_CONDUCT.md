# RetroChat Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

RetroChat is committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and inclusive environment for everyone — regardless of age, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful.** Treat every user with dignity and kindness.
- **Be constructive.** Offer helpful feedback; avoid personal attacks.
- **Keep it appropriate.** Do not share explicit, violent, or disturbing content.
- **Respect privacy.** Never share another user's personal information without consent.
- **Follow the rules.** Each chat room and community may have additional guidelines set by its moderators.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on RetroChat:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
- Hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory language
- Sharing illegal content, including pirated material
- Spam, scam links, or phishing attempts
- Impersonating other users, moderators, or administrators
- Doxxing or sharing personally identifiable information
- Exploiting platform features for malicious purposes
- Attempting to manipulate token markets or mislead users about financial products

## Age Requirement

RetroChat requires all users to be at least **13 years of age**. Certain features (such as Web3 wallet integration and token creation) may require users to be **18 or older**. Age verification is enforced at sign-up.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. **Warning** — A moderator or admin will notify you of the violation.
2. **Muting** — Your ability to send messages may be temporarily restricted.
3. **Temporary Ban** — You may be removed from a room or the platform for a set period.
4. **Permanent Ban** — Severe or repeated violations will result in permanent removal.

Moderators and Community Leaders have the authority to enforce these rules within their rooms and communities. Platform-wide enforcement is handled by RetroChat administrators.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation, please use the **Bug Report** or contact a moderator directly. All reports are reviewed promptly and handled confidentially.

## Financial Disclaimer

RetroChat provides tools for creating and interacting with Solana-based tokens. These tools are provided as-is and do not constitute financial advice. Users are solely responsible for their own financial decisions. RetroChat does not endorse, guarantee, or take responsibility for any token, airdrop, or transaction conducted on the platform.
