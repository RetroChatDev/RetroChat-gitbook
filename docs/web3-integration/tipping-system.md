# Tipping System

Show appreciation and support other users with RetroChat's tipping system.

## How to Send Tips

### From a User Profile

1. Visit the user's profile
2. Click the "Tip" button
3. Enter the amount
4. Select the token (SOL or native token)
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
6. Tip is sent!

### From a Chat Message

1. Hover over a message
2. Click the tip icon
3. Enter the amount
4. Confirm in your wallet
5. Tip is sent instantly

### Quick Tips

- Use preset amounts for quick tipping
- Set custom amounts
- Add a message with your tip (optional)

## Receiving Tips

### Notification
- Get notified when you receive a tip
- See tip amount and sender
- View in your activity feed

### Withdrawal
- Tips are sent directly to your connected wallet
- No withdrawal needed
- Check your wallet balance

### History
- View all received tips
- See tip statistics
- Track your earnings

## Tipping Best Practices

1. **Tip Quality Content**: Reward users who contribute value
2. **Be Generous**: Even small tips are appreciated
3. **Add Messages**: Personalize your tips with messages
4. **Regular Tipping**: Support creators regularly

## Tipping Etiquette

- Tip amounts are entirely up to you
- No pressure to tip
- Tips are public by default (can be made private)
- Respectful tipping only

## Fees

- Small network fee (paid in SOL)
- No platform fees
- Transparent fee structure
- Fees shown before confirmation

## Privacy

### Public Tips
- Visible on user profiles
- Shown in activity feeds
- Displayed in chat (optional)

### Private Tips
- Only visible to sender and receiver
- Not shown in public feeds
- Complete privacy option

## Limits

- Minimum tip: 0.001 SOL
- Maximum tip: No limit
- Daily limits may apply for new accounts

## Tax Considerations

- Tips may be subject to tax regulations
- Consult a tax professional
- Keep records of transactions
- RetroChat does not provide tax advice

## Troubleshooting

**Tip not sent?**
- Check your wallet balance
- Verify transaction was approved
- Check network status

**Tip not received?**
- Verify wallet is connected
- Check transaction on blockchain explorer
- Contact support if issue persists

**Wrong amount?**
- Double-check before confirming
- Contact support immediately if error
- Transactions cannot be reversed
