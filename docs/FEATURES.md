# RetroChat Features

A complete overview of everything RetroChat has to offer.

---

## 💬 Live Chat Rooms

The heart of RetroChat. Create and join real-time chat rooms with a classic internet vibe.

- **Create rooms** — Public or private, with optional password protection
- **Customize** — Set room descriptions, tags, wallpapers, and max user limits
- **Rich messaging** — Emoticons, GIFs, file attachments, image sharing, and message replies
- **Mentions** — Tag other users with `@username` to get their attention
- **Message moderation** — Room creators and moderators can delete messages
- **Favorites** — Star your favorite rooms for quick access
- **Room search** — Find rooms by name or tag

---

## 🌐 Communities

Communities are shared spaces for groups of people with common interests.

- **Create a community** — Give it a name, description, banner image, and social links
- **Community posts** — Share updates, discussions, and media with members
- **Post interactions** — Like, reply, pin, and report posts
- **Member management** — View members, assign roles, moderate content
- **Community visibility** — Public or invite-only communities

---

## 🎙️ Spaces (Voice Rooms)

Real-time audio spaces for live conversations within communities.

- **Start a Space** — Community owners and moderators can launch voice rooms
- **Join and listen** — Any community member can tune in
- **Request to speak** — Listeners can raise their hand to join the conversation
- **Speaker management** — Hosts can promote listeners, revoke speaking privileges, and manage the stage
- **Co-hosts** — Invite trusted members to help moderate the Space

---

## 📡 Live Broadcasting

Go live within your community with video or audio broadcasts.

- **Browser-based streaming** — Broadcast directly from your browser with camera and microphone
- **Camera preview** — Preview your camera before going live
- **Live playback** — Community members can watch the broadcast in real time
- **OBS integration** — Advanced streamers can connect OBS via WebSocket for professional broadcasts
- **RTMP relay** — Stream to external platforms simultaneously

---

## 🪙 Token Launchpad (Token Studio)

Create and launch your own Solana token directly from RetroChat.

- **Token creation wizard** — Set name, ticker, description, and supply
- **Image upload** — Add a token logo with IPFS-backed storage
- **Launch platforms** — Choose between pump.fun and Raydium for token launch
- **Token gallery** — Browse tokens created by the RetroChat community
- **Token comments** — Discuss tokens with other users
- **Launch status tracking** — Monitor your token through creation, pending, and launched stages

---

## 📺 Retro TV

Watch curated retro content right inside RetroChat.

- **Themed channels** — Browse channels organized by category (cartoons, music, gaming, etc.)
- **Channel groups** — Channels are organized into logical groups for easy browsing
- **TV Guide** — See what's on and browse the schedule
- **Minimized player** — Keep watching while you chat with the mini footer player
- **Expanded player** — Full-screen viewing experience
- **Content sources** — Videos from YouTube, Vimeo, Internet Archive, and more
- **IPTV integration** — Access live TV streams from around the world

---

## 📻 Live Radio

Listen to live radio stations from around the world while you chat.

- **Station browser** — Search and browse thousands of radio stations
- **Genre and country filters** — Find stations by music genre or location
- **Now playing** — See what's currently playing on your selected station
- **Background playback** — Radio keeps playing as you navigate the app
- **iHeart integration** — Access iHeart Radio stations

---

## 💸 Tipping

Send and receive SOL tips to show appreciation.

- **Tip any user** — Send SOL directly to another user's connected wallet
- **Custom amounts** — Choose how much to tip
- **Tip history** — View your sent and received tips
- **Tip leaderboard** — See the most generous tippers and most tipped users
- **Real-time notifications** — Get notified when you receive a tip

---

## 🎁 Airdrops

Run token airdrop campaigns for your community.

- **Create campaigns** — Set up airdrops with specific tokens and amounts
- **Recipient targeting** — Airdrop to room members, specific users, or custom criteria
- **Campaign tracking** — Monitor successful and failed sends
- **Campaign management** — View, execute, and cancel campaigns

---

## 🔗 Web3 Integration

RetroChat is built with Web3 at its core.

- **Wallet connection** — Connect Phantom or Solflare wallets
- **DeFi portfolio** — View your token holdings and positions
- **NFT gallery** — Browse your NFT collection
- **Price alerts** — Set alerts for token price movements
- **Transaction history** — View your on-chain activity
- **IPFS file storage** — Upload and pin files to the decentralized web

See [Web3 Integration](./WEB3_INTEGRATION.md) for full details.
