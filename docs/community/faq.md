# FAQ

Frequently asked questions about RetroChat.

## General Questions

### What is RetroChat?
RetroChat is a modern social platform combining retro chat room aesthetics with Web3 technology, built on Solana.

### How do I create an account?
Visit RetroChat and click "Sign Up". You can use email, Google OAuth, or connect a Solana wallet.

### Is RetroChat free?
Yes, RetroChat is free to use. Some premium features may require tokens.

### What browsers are supported?
RetroChat works on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

## Account Questions

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Can I change my username?
Yes, you can change your username in account settings. Some restrictions may apply.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This action is permanent.

## Features Questions

### How do I create a room?
Navigate to Rooms, click "Create Room", fill in details, and create your room.

### Can I send files?
Yes, you can attach files to messages. Supported formats and size limits apply.

### How do I tip someone?
Visit their profile and click "Tip", or use the tip button on messages.

## Web3 Questions

### Which wallets are supported?
Currently Phantom and Solflare are supported. More wallets coming soon.

### How do I connect my wallet?
Go to Settings → Wallet and click "Connect Wallet". Follow the prompts.

### Are there fees?
Small network fees apply for blockchain transactions. No platform fees for tipping.

### How do I receive airdrops?
Airdrops are automatically sent to your connected wallet. Check your airdrop history.

## Technical Questions

### Why can't I connect my wallet?
Make sure your wallet extension is installed and unlocked. Try refreshing the page.

### Messages not loading?
Check your internet connection, refresh the page, or clear your browser cache.

### Platform is slow?
This could be due to high traffic. Try refreshing or check our status page.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

### What data do you collect?
We collect data necessary to provide our services. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Can I make my profile private?
Yes, you can adjust privacy settings in your account settings.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't log in?
Verify your credentials, try password reset, or contact support.

### Feature not working?
Check if the feature is available, try refreshing, or report the issue.

### Need more help?
Visit our [Support](./support.md) page or contact support directly.
