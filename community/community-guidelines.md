# Community Guidelines

Guidelines for participating in the RetroChat community.

## General Guidelines

### Be Respectful
- Treat all members with respect
- Use appropriate language
- Avoid personal attacks
- Be constructive

### Be Helpful
- Help newcomers
- Share knowledge
- Answer questions
- Support others

### Be Positive
- Maintain positive attitude
- Encourage others
- Celebrate successes
- Build community

## Content Guidelines

### Appropriate Content
- Keep content appropriate
- Respect others' boundaries
- Consider your audience
- Think before posting

### Prohibited Content
- Illegal content
- Explicit material
- Hate speech
- Harassment

### Spam & Self-Promotion
- No excessive self-promotion
- Relevant content only
- Follow promotion rules
- Respect community space

## Communication

### Effective Communication
- Be clear and concise
- Use appropriate tone
- Ask questions
- Provide context

### Disagreements
- Stay respectful
- Focus on issues
- Avoid personal attacks
- Seek resolution

## Room Guidelines

### Room Rules
- Follow room-specific rules
- Respect room moderators
- Stay on topic
- Contribute meaningfully

### Room Creation
- Create appropriate rooms
- Set clear rules
- Moderate effectively
- Maintain quality

## Moderation

### Moderator Authority
- Respect moderator decisions
- Follow moderator instructions
- Appeal process available
- Fair enforcement

### Reporting
- Report violations
- Provide evidence
- Use reporting system
- Trust the process

## Consequences

### Violation Consequences
- Warnings
- Temporary restrictions
- Suspension
- Permanent ban

### Appeal Process
- Request review
- Provide information
- Fair consideration
- Final decision
